# 🔒 Privacy Policy – Creators Eye Battery Reporter

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Our Commitment
At Creators Eye, we believe privacy is a fundamental right.  
**This tool collects NO data. Nothing leaves your device.**

---

## What Data Is Stored?

| Data | Where It Goes | Why |
|------|---------------|-----|
| Battery percentage | Stays in your browser's memory | For the test |
| Test logs (timestamps, percentages) | Saved to your chosen folder or Downloads as a text file | So you have a record |
| Folder preference | Your browser's IndexedDB | So you don't have to pick a folder every time |
| Terms acceptance | Your browser's localStorage | So you only see the terms once |
| Test history (reports) | Your browser's localStorage | To show previous reports and comparison charts |

All stored data is **local to your device**. We never have access to it.

---

## What Data Is NOT Collected

❌ No personal information (name, email, address)  
❌ No IP addresses  
❌ No tracking cookies  
❌ No analytics scripts  
❌ No cloud uploads  
❌ Nothing sent to Creators Eye servers  

---

## Third-Party Libraries

The browser tool loads the following libraries from public CDNs:

- **Chart.js** – for drawing discharge charts
- **JSZip** – for creating ZIP files in browsers without folder support
- **FileSaver.js** – for saving ZIP files

These libraries run entirely in your browser. **No data is transmitted to their servers** – the libraries are fetched once and then execute locally.

If you prefer to use the tool completely offline, you can download these libraries manually and host them locally, but the tool is designed to work offline after the first load.

---

## Your Control

✅ You choose where reports are saved (folder or Downloads).  
✅ You can clear your browser data to remove saved preferences and history.  
✅ You can delete any saved reports from your computer at any time.  
✅ You can disconnect from the internet – the tool still works.
